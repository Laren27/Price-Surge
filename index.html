Total observations across 19 restaurants
Dynamic pricing on Zomato is highly concentrated — Faasos alone accounts for the majority of all price change events (167 of 214), suggesting the practice is brand-level policy, not a platform-wide norm.
Despite popular belief, rain and temperature have no statistically significant effect on restaurant pricing in this market — confirmed via Mann-Whitney U and Kruskal-Wallis tests (p > 0.05 across all 19 restaurants).
Weekend pricing premiums exist but are restaurant-specific, not market-wide — Wow! Momo shows an 18.96% weekend uplift while most competitors remain fully static.
